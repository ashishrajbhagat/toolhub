Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how MyToolKitPro protects your data.

1. File Processing

All file processing (PDF conversion, merging, image processing) happens directly in yourbrowser. MyToolKitPro does not upload, store, or access your files on any server.

Your documents remain completely on your device at all times.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, address, or payment details. Our tools can be used without registration.

7. Policy Updates

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
