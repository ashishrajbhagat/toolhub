Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how ToolHub protects your data.

1. File Processing

All file processing (PDF conversion, merging, image processing) happens directly in your browser. ToolHub does not upload, store, or access your files on any server.

Your documents remain completely on your device at all times.

2. Personal Information

We do not collect personal information such as your name, address, or payment details. Our tools can be used without registration.

3. Cookies

ToolHub may use cookies to improve user experience and analyze website traffic. Cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

We use Google Analytics to monitor website performance and visitor behavior. This may include browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.

5. Advertising

In the future, ToolHub may display advertisements through services such as Google AdSense. These services may use cookies to show relevant ads.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
