Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how MyToolKitPro protects your data.

1. Local File Processing

All file processing (including PDF conversion, merging, and image manipulation) happens directly in your browser. MyToolKitPro does not upload, store, or access your files on any server.

Your documents remain completely on your device at all times.

2. Personal Information

We do not collect personal information such as your name, address, or payment details. Our tools are completely free to use without registration.

3. Analytics & Tracking

To understand how our website is used and to improve your experience, we use standard Google Analytics (GA4). This service collects basic, anonymous usage data such as your browser type, device category, geographical region, and which tools you click on.

Crucially, our analytics tracking has absolutely zero access to the files you process. We track that a tool was used, but we never see the contents or names of the files you are using it on.

4. Cookies

MyToolKitPro uses cookies primarily to facilitate our Google Analytics integration. These text files help us analyze overall website traffic patterns. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, and all of our tools will still function perfectly.

5. Third-Party Links & Advertising

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. Currently, MyToolKitPro does not run third-party advertisements, but if we partner with ad networks (like Google AdSense) in the future, this policy will be updated.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
